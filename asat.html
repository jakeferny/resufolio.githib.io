AlbertaSat

Mission Design Team Lead

2021 - 2024

Edmonton, Alberta


AlbertaSat is a student CubeSat satellite team at the University of Alberta and is responsible for ideating, developing, and operating Ex-Alta and Ex-Alta 2, the first two Alberta built satellites in orbit. Now we are currently working on the third iteration, Ex-Alta 3 which is set to launch in 2026.


My involvment with AlbertaSat started in 2021. Since then I have held a major role with two different teams; Mechanical, and Mission Design. My work on the Mechanical Team consisted primarily of design for a weather balloon chasis and performing buoyancy calculations for the Dedicated Electron Balloon Observatory for Radiation Belt Analysis (DEBORA) mission. Read more about my involvment on the team here and more about the mission itself here.


In 2023 I took the lessons I learned from working with the satellite team at Calian and made a jump over to the Mission Design team, where my experiences gained me the the position of deputy team lead. In a nutshell, the Mission Design team is responsible for the overall planning and execution of deliverables set for Ex-Alta 3. See more about my work in this role on Ex-Alta 3 here. I am currently the Mission Design team lead, where I work with the team to ensure mission obejctives are met.
